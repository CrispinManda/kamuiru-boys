THE BOARD CHAIRMAN-CPA
Kennedy Muriithi Ngiabi.

The current chairman is serving his second term as chair. He is an alumni of Kamuiru boys high school. He is a professional accountant and the immediate former chairperson of Kirinyaga County Public service Board where he served for a historic 6 year term uninterrupted. He is most focused and passionate about the growth of the school. His infrastructure development blueprint is evident in form of tuition blocks, Two Storey Dormitory, Kei-apple fence, Cash crops and Pigs project as income generating initiatives among other notable projects His Board of management is targeting a 100% transition to the Universities.

THE PA CHAIRMAN
Mr Peter Ng’ang’a

He is professionally a qualified conservator with the Kenya Forest Service in charge. His passion for trees and tree cover is overwhelming. He was elected unanimously by all parents in 2023. His 3 sons have gone through Kamuiru boys high school successful to the University. His last born son is in school now F3.

THE CHIEF PRINCIPAL-'
Dr Nixon K Njue PhD Chrp-k.

The chief Principal was posted to the school in the year 2020 after the successful service and retirement of the former immediate Principal Mr Stephen M. Machina. The principal teaches Kiswahili and Geography. He has a vast wealth of experience having been a principal for the last 27 years uninterrupted. He fully derives pleasure with the interaction of boys and has cut a niche in their process of their becoming responsible men. His Moulding, mentorship and coaching is unmatched. His soft skills in administration is led by ""The Buck Stops Here"" conspicuously hanged on his door. His life mantra has been "My Duty, My Signature"

DEPUTY PRINCIPAL ACADEMICS
Festus K. Wachira-deputy principal academics

Leading academic excellence: As Academic Deputy Principal, I spearhead initiatives to ensure a thriving learning environment. My focus lies on curriculum development, instructional leadership, teacher supervision, and student assessment. Collaboration is key, working with staff, students, and the broader community. Our goal? To empower students through a modern, engaging curriculum and foster a culture of research and innovation among teachers.

Mr Godfrey I. Ruiru-deputy principal administration

Ensuring a smooth school operation: As the Administrative Deputy Principal, I oversee daily operations and student discipline at our school with over 1,500 students. My role involves curriculum implementation, fostering a positive learning environment, and maintaining student well-being. We achieve this through strong relationships between teachers and students, along with a multi-faceted approach. This includes academic support programs, parent and alumni involvement, practical learning experiences, and dedicated exam preparation initiatives
